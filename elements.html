Awards and Recognitions

Neeya Naana Award

Recognized by Gopinath for an enlightening debate on modernity and its impact on Tamil Nadu society, sparking national dialogue on pressing social issues. The debate focused on the challenges of modernity and its implications for traditional values, resonating deeply with viewers across the country. I discussed how older generations often perceive modernity as merely a superficial change in physical appearance, while neglecting its profound impact on societal norms and values.

Tamil Nadu Government's Award for Excellence in Public Speaking

Secured second place at the Kalaingar 100 event, competing against top orators from various colleges in Kanchipuram district, Tamil Nadu. I demonstrated exceptional skill in delivering compelling speeches that emphasized the importance of youth empowerment and community engagement. My speech inspired action among young leaders, fostering a sense of responsibility towards societal issues.

Thiru Vi Ka Award for Essay Writing

Honored for outstanding essay writing on environmental issues, promoting civic engagement and positive change. The essay highlighted sustainable development practices and their crucial role in shaping a greener future for Tamil Nadu. It encouraged public participation in environmental conservation efforts, emphasizing the need for collective action to preserve our natural resources.

Makkal TV Best Speaker Award

Celebrated for captivating audiences with thought-provoking narratives and persuasive arguments, particularly addressing farmers' issues on platforms like Uzhavar Medai. My speech shed light on the challenges faced by farmers and proposed innovative solutions to improve agricultural practices and rural livelihoods. It aimed to raise awareness about the importance of sustainable farming practices and equitable agricultural policies.

These awards highlight my journey as a public speaker, reinforcing my commitment to leveraging words for positive social change and inspiring meaningful dialogue within communities.
